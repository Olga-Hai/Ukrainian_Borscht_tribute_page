Ukrainian Borscht

The main dish of Ukrainian cuisine

What does the word borscht mean?

: a soup made primarily of beets and served hot or cold often with sour cream.

Why is borscht healthy?

The main ingredient of borscht, beetroot, helps to create more red blood cells and cleanse the blood. Beets are very useful for curing and treating anemia. ... You can enjoy the blood cleansing health benefits associated with beet when you eat borscht, which is why you should include this superfood in your diet weekly!

Interesting Facts About Borsch To Know:

  • Borsch (борщ in Cyrillic)
  • It's a daily staple in the Ukrainian kitchen
  • Usually coloured red from beetroot
  • Beets are cooked separately
  • Potatoes and tomatoes began being added to borsch in the 19th century
  • A special flavor of borsch comes from garlic and bacon
